Product Description

The Eternity Diamond Ring is a timeless symbol of everlasting love and commitment. Crafted by our master jewelers, this ring features a flawless row of brilliant-cut diamonds that encircle the entire band, creating an endless sparkle.

Each diamond is meticulously set in a secure four-prong setting to maximize light reflection and ensure durability. The 18k white gold band has a polished finish that complements the diamonds' brilliance.

Perfect for anniversaries, engagements, or as a special gift, this ring comes with a premium velvet presentation box and a lifetime warranty on craftsmanship.
